History of Economics on the Web

History of Economic Thought: http://www.hetwebsite.net/het/ (biographies of economists and descriptions of schools of economic thought)

Economists' Portraits:http://hope.econ.duke.edu/media/economists-portraits (pictures of economists)

Library of Economics and Liberty: http://www.econlib.org/ (free books online)

Liberty Fund: http://www.libertyfund.org/ (really cheap classics)

McMaster Archive: http://historyofeconomicthought.mcmaster.ca/ (free works online from little known economists and philosophers)

History of Economics Society: http://www.historyofeconomics.org/ (academic organization for historians of economics)

I moderate the SHOE (Societies for the History of Economic Thought) email discussion list and you can look at the archives here:

https://listserv.yorku.ca/archives/shoe.html
